Our Books

Superkathy and the Southern Trapezoid

R. T. Vale

Superkathy has sworn to use her powers for the good of humanity. Her friend and housemate Dr. Squiddy is the world's most brilliant squid-headed scientist. A request for help from the Tanzanian wildlife service catapults them into a thrilling globetrotting adventure. Risking life and limb, they discover that behind it all looms a dark, murderous force which threatens to destroy not only London, but the fabric of time and space itself! But that is not all. Because someone has big plans for Superkathy…
